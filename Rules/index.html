Rules for visiting the chain of cinemas"Monroe Cinema"

  1. The cinema ticket office opens 15 minutes before the start of the first session and closes 15 minutes after the start of the last session
  2. Purchased tickets are valid strictly during the hours and days indicated on the ticket
  3. To purchase a student or school card, you must present the appropriate document
  4. When entering the cinemas, the guest must present a ticket, as well as a student card or a certificate from the school
  5. 3D glasses designed for watching RealD 3D movies
  6. The following ticket categories are valid in the cinema:
  • Child ticket - for children from 5 to 15 years old inclusive
  • Children over the age of 12, in order to purchase a child ticket, must have a school certificate or birth certificate with them.
  • Student ID - for guests holding a registered, valid student ID
  • Adult - for guests aged 16 and over
  • The administration of the cinema has the right to partially or completely change or cancel the actions of some categories of tickets
